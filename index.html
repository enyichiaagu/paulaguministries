OUR OUTREACHES
SOPHIA BOOK TOURS
Mobilizes the society to read and educate the mind via good books and magazines.

CTAN CRUSADES
(Christ To All Nations) is an evangelistic platform, that takes God's word to the people via mass crusades,revivals, retreats etc.

YOUNG LIFE INT'L
It is an outreach that caters for the youths/kids in schools, campuses, prisons, touts in motorparks, homeless kidsand drug addicts.

PIONEERS
This is the mission outreach that takes the church to the un-churched and unreached nations via prayers, compassion and church planting strategies. It includes both local and foreign mission trips.

SOPHIA S.O.M.
Sophia School of Mission is a bible based missionary/ministry training institute. It usually lasts for four weeks-twice yearly. It is packaged to equip the body of christ with the winning strategies to fufil the great commission.

IGNITE THE FIRE CONVENTION
This is a bienial camping retreat and convention. Here, God's word is made to come alive to refresh souls in the power of the holy spirit.

WoV-WORD, WORSHIP AND PRAYER CONVENTION
The 'Women of Valour' is the brain-child of Evangelist Esther Paul Agu. This forum exposes women to the rhema of the word, and winnable prayer formulas, and the secret power on worship.

OPERATION SMILE
Operation Smile is the compassion-arm of Paul Agu Ministries. It caters for the material, financial, emotional and health needs of the poor, sick and vulnerable women and children in the society.

MEDIA-SOPHIA
This department houses the ministy's periodical publications; and they are distributed 'Free of Charge' to our teeming patners. 'THE DOVE' doubles the ministry's news letter.

FRANK TALK
It is a seminar, Conference and summit where participants are inspired, motivated and spurred on to be the best in all life's endeavours. Frank Talk is fiery and scintillating, thanks to the eloquence and 'word-smitting' prowess of the chief speaker.

WORD CONFERENCES
These are expository, didactic lectures based on the holy bible. Word Conferences last between an hour to three hours. Each Conference can take a day to three days.
